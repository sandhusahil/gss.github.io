Welcome to Gurudwara Singh Sabha, Bhalla Colony, Chheharta

Gurudwara Singh Sabha, situated in the heart of Bhalla Colony, Chheharta, is a sacred haven that embodies the spiritual essence of Sikhism. Rooted in a rich cultural heritage, the Gurudwara stands as a symbol of devotion, community, and service. Its serene atmosphere resonates with the divine teachings of Gurbani, creating a tranquil space for worshippers to connect with their spirituality.

At Gurudwara Singh Sabha, we are dedicated to fostering unity and inclusivity. Our daily prayers, Kirtan sessions, and the practice of Langar exemplify our commitment to selfless service and communal harmony. The Gurudwara welcomes individuals from all walks of life, transcending boundaries of caste, color, and creed.

Join us in the vibrant events and celebrations that unfold within our sacred walls. From religious festivals to community gatherings and charitable initiatives, Gurudwara Singh Sabha is a hub of cultural richness and social welfare. Our commitment extends beyond the spiritual realm to actively engage in uplifting the local community.

Surrounded by lush greenery and echoing with the soothing sounds of prayers, Gurudwara Singh Sabha is not just a physical structure but a spiritual anchor, inspiring individuals to live harmoniously and embody the principles of Sikhism. We invite you to be a part of our community and experience the divine teachings encapsulated within our sacred Gurudwara.
